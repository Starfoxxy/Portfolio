About Me

Hello!

I'm Athena Jacob, a driven full-stack developer committed to changing the world through technology.

I specialize in technologies such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, and Node.js.

From a young age, I’ve been captivated by the ever-evolving fields of robotics and programming, with a firm belief that we can harness these technologies to solve some of the most pressing challenges facing our society. My goal is to not only build software, but to use it as a tool for transformation. I believe technology should be used to empower the vulnerable and to give people access to the information they need, when they need it.

Whether it’s providing vulnerable communities with a secure platform to voice their experiences, supporting victims of violence in sharing their stories in a safe space, tracking abuses within the system, or creating platforms that educate the youth, I am determined to be part of the change. Technology has the power to amplify these voices, giving individuals the power to share their stories, access support, and empower individuals to reclaim their lives.

Let’s change the world together, one line of code at a time.

Digital Marketing Performance Dashboard

This data-driven dashboard was developed for a digital marketing company to streamline campaign management, optimize client performance, and drive higher revenue through detailed analytics and actionable insights. Utilizing React for the frontend and Node.js with Express for the backend, the platform aggregates and visualizes key performance indicators (KPIs) such as conversion rates, customer acquisition costs, return on ad spend (ROAS), and overall ROI, offering a centralized hub for monitoring marketing efforts across multiple channels. Data is stored in MongoDB, ensuring scalability, and JWT authentication is used to secure access.

Social Media Analytics Tool

This comprehensive social media analytics platform is designed to help influencers and brands monitor, analyze, and optimize their social media performance across multiple platforms. Built with React for the frontend and Node.js with Express for the backend, the platform integrates APIs from Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook to provide real-time data on follower growth, engagement rates, content performance, and audience demographics. Data is securely stored in MongoDB, ensuring both security and scalability, with JWT authentication to protect user access. By utilizing Chart.js for data visualizations, users can track metrics like impressions, clicks, likes, comments, shares, and overall engagement rates.
